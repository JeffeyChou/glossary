# 1

$$\begin{pmatrix}3 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & -8 & -2 \\ 1 & 1 & 5\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}u \\ v \\ w\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}-2 \\ 1 \\ 4\end{pmatrix}$$

  • Jacobi $$\begin{align*} \begin{pmatrix}u_{k+1} \\ v_{k+1} \\ w_{k+1}\end{pmatrix}&=\begin{pmatrix}3 \\ & -8 \\ & & 5\end{pmatrix}^{-1}\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 0 & 2 \\ -1 & -1 & 0\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}u_{k} \\ v_{k} \\ w_{k}\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}3 \\ & -8 \\ & & 5\end{pmatrix}^{-1}\begin{pmatrix}-2 \\ 1 \\ 4\end{pmatrix}\\ &=\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1/3 & -1/3\\ 1/8 & 0 & -1/4\\ -1/5 & -1/5 & 0\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}u_{k} \\ v_{k} \\ w_{k}\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}-2/3\\ -5/40\\ 4/5\end{pmatrix} \end{align*}$$

  • Gauss-Seidel $$\begin{align*} \begin{pmatrix}u_{k+1} \\ v_{k+1} \\ w_{k+1}\end{pmatrix}&=\begin{pmatrix}3\\ 1 & -8\\ 1 & 1 & 5\end{pmatrix}^{-1}\begin{pmatrix} 0& 1 & -1\\ 0& 0 & 2\\ 0& 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}.\begin{pmatrix}u_{k} \\ v_{k} \\ w_{k}\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}3\\ 1 & -8\\ 1 & 1 & 5\end{pmatrix}^{-1}\begin{pmatrix}-2 \\ 1\\ 4\end{pmatrix}\\ &=\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1/3 & -1/3\\ 1/8 & 1/24 & -7/24\\ -9/40 & -11/40 & 1/8\end{pmatrix}.\begin{pmatrix}u_{k} \\ v_{k} \\ w_{k}\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}-2/3\\ -5/24\\ 39/40\end{pmatrix} \end{align*}$$
